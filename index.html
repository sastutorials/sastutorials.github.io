About this website

Welcome!
This website has been created to offer you a friendly environment where to learn programming with SAS. This website is meant for beginners and whoever needs to re-fresh their knowledge of SAS.

WHAT TO EXPECT?

Here is what you can expect to gain by following this learning journey with us on SAS!

You will learn SAS by working your way through a series of tutorials, from the basics of SAS to more advanced programming. The tutorials are grouped according to a macro topic, so that it is easier for you to navigate them. Each tutorial covers a specific topic in the programming realm of SAS.
The tutorials are structured and organised in clear sections, each led by theoretical explanations and followed by interactive visualisations and examples, to help you gain a full understanding of the subject matter.
We promise you it won't be boring! You'll be actively interacting with the tutorials, as you will have the data and code available to program on your own while you follow the tutorial along. We are going to use one sample dataset across the various tutorials to help you familiarise with it, and make your learning journey feel like that of an actual data scientist!
In addition, you'll have plenty of opportunities to practice and test yourself with solo exercises, available at the end of each tutorial. Solutions to the exercises will also be available for you to check after.
We hope you'll enjoy this learning experience, and will fulfill your objective of learning and feeling confident with SAS programming!


LEARNING OBJECTIVES

This is a summary of the learning objectives from this educational journey:
  • Become familiar with SAS Software and one of its platforms: SAS Studio;

  • Learn how to easily detect errors and quickly debug programs;

  • Become a confident programmer;

  • Learn data importing and manipulation;

  • Learn statistics and modelling;

  • Create great visualisations and informative graphs, and learn how to report your work efficiently and concisely.
